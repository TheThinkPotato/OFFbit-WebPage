Computer Tech Stuff.

BSEL Mod.

The Tape BSEL Mod. A mod for the core 2 CPUs that changes the FSB clock by tricking the motherbaord to think it's CPU runs at a higher FSB. Giving us an overclock, This is a good way to overclock OEM PCs that have no overclocking control in their BIOS.

LGA 771 to 775 Mod.

This mod will enable LGA 771 CPUs into a standard LGA 775 motherbaord. This mod requires hardware and bios mods for it to work.

HP IPISB-CU New GPU Work Around.

HP IPISB-CU has some serious issues with newer hardware. This workaround will enable you to get a NVidia 700 series or newer card to work on this PC.
